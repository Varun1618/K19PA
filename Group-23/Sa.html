Stamp Albums

Stamp albums are a variable number of products traditionally employed by stamp collectors and philatelists in mounting, displaying, and storing their stamp collections. Many excellent, high quality, beautiful albums are made today to suit the interests of all types of collectors and specialists. Unfortunately, many of them have gotten very expensive. Should you prefer to store your collection in a manufactured album, there are plenty of great choices. And of course, every stamp collector wants to be able to "show-off" their treasures to their family and friends in the most elegant way that is possible.

There are also alternative choices, such as displaying your collection in stock books (less expensive than albums), or on stock pages designed to fit in a loose leaf binder. Many collectors and specialists would rather spend the $500 or $1,000 they've saved up for that exquisite stamp they've always wanted on the stamp instead of on the book in which to mount it.
